Features

Built for modern startups. Designed to scale with you from day one to 500+ employees. Every feature intentionally crafted to help teams move faster.

Decision Tracking

Never lose context again. Document every important decision, track stakeholder input, and build an institutional memory that scales with your team. Stop asking "why did we decide that?" six months later.

  • Structured decision framework with context, options, and outcomes
  • Stakeholder voting and async collaboration
  • Full decision history and searchable archive
  • Automatic notifications to relevant team members
  • Link decisions to tasks and projects

Smart Task Management

Tasks that auto-prioritize based on deadlines, dependencies, and team capacity. See what matters right now, not buried in an endless list. Built for teams that ship fast.

  • Auto-prioritization based on urgency and impact
  • Smart dependencies and blocking task detection
  • Team capacity planning and workload balancing
  • Recurring tasks with flexible schedules
  • Time tracking and velocity metrics

Company Announcements

Broadcast company-wide updates that actually get read. Track who's seen what, pin critical updates, and ensure nothing important gets lost in Slack noise.

  • Rich text editor with embeds, images, and videos
  • Read receipts and engagement analytics
  • Pin important announcements to the top
  • Schedule announcements for optimal timing
  • Category tags for filtering (company news, wins, product updates)

People Operations

HR essentials without the enterprise bloat. Time off requests, onboarding workflows, team directory, and org charts. Everything you need to manage people as you scale.

  • Time off management with team calendar visibility
  • Customizable onboarding checklists and workflows
  • Interactive org chart with team structures
  • Role-based permissions and access control
  • Employee profiles with skills and contact info

Analytics Dashboard

See what's working. Track team velocity, decision turnaround time, task completion rates, and engagement metrics. Data-driven insights without the complexity.

  • Real-time team velocity and throughput metrics
  • Decision-making speed and quality tracking
  • Individual and team performance analytics
  • Custom reports and data exports
  • Weekly digest emails with key insights

Widget Integrations

Connect your existing tools. Slack, Gmail, and Outlook widgets let you create tasks, log decisions, and capture important moments without leaving your workflow.

  • Slack widget: Turn messages into tasks and decisions
  • Gmail integration: Email to task conversion
  • Outlook add-in: Full Startup OS access from inbox
  • Browser extension for quick capture anywhere
  • API access for custom integrations
