Frequently asked
questions

Do I need to replace my existing tools?

No. StartUp OS integrates with Slack, Gmail, and Outlook as widgets. You can keep your existing workflow and just use StartUp OS to capture decisions, track tasks, and manage people ops. We're designed to complement your stack, not replace it.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. If you upgrade mid-billing cycle, we'll prorate the difference. If you downgrade, the change takes effect at your next billing date. No penalties or fees for changes.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! The Starter plan is free forever for up to 10 people. The Growth plan includes a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. Enterprise plans can request a custom trial period.

What happens when I exceed my plan limits?

If you exceed your team size on the Starter plan, we'll notify you and give you 14 days to upgrade. Your data is never locked or deleted. For storage limits, we'll alert you when you reach 90% capacity.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or students?

Yes! We offer 50% off Growth plans for registered nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact our sales team with proof of status to apply for the discount.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) and ACH transfers for annual plans. Enterprise customers can pay via invoice with NET 30 terms.

Can I export my data if I leave?

Absolutely. You can export all your data at any time in standard formats (JSON, CSV). No lock-in, no hassle. We believe your data should always be portable.

What kind of support do you provide?

Starter plans get email support with 24-hour response times. Growth plans get priority email and chat support. Enterprise plans include a dedicated account manager and phone support.
